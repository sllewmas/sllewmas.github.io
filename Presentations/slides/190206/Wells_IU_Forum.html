Strange Pilgrims

  • Ongoing project of a set of works for trumpet and interactive electronics.
  • Each work is based on a short story from Strange Pilgrims by Gabriel García Márquez
  • The first work in the set is:

Light is like Water

Light is like Water

  • Two brothers discover that they can fill their room with light by turning on a faucet, as one can do with water. This liquid light becomes a surreal environment in which they swim and dive.
  • The musical interpretation of this experience begins with volatile, electric sparks of energy that cohere into a glassy and fluid texture that trumpeter explores.

The Lacuna

  • 32 minute work for piano four-hands and interactive electronics
  • written for HOCKET and made possible by a grant from the ACF and the Jerome Foundation

  • based on Barbara Kingsolver’s novel of the same title.
  • The novel is comprised of the diaries of Harrison William Shepherd chronicling his entire life.
  • But there are significant gaps in the diaries, which compels the reader to construct periods of Shepherd’s life.
